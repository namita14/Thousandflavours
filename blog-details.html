Preparation time: 5 minutes Cooking time : 5 minutes Serves : 4-6 1 cup = 250 ml cup Storage : You can store the hummus in a clean, odour free jar/bowl and keep it refrigerated for uptown 2 days. You can also line the jar/vessel with olive oil before you transfer the hummus for longer shelf life.
Ingredients : 1 cup green gram sprouts 1 beetroot 2 garlic cloves 1 dry red chilli (adjust) juice of 2 lemons 1-2 teaspoon sesame oil 1 tablespoon olive oil ( adjust) salt to taste
For garnish: 1/4 teaspoon roasted cumin powder/jeera powder 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds (toasted) 1-2 teaspoon olive oil
